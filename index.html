How to Play

1. Bet on a Color: Red, Green, or Violet.
2. Or Bet on a Number (0-9).
3. Each round is 30 seconds: 25s to bet, 5s for result.
4. 2% service fee is deducted.
5. Red/Green pays 2x, Violet pays 4.5x, Numbers pay 9x.
6. Result determines win/loss.
7. Withdraw anytime.
